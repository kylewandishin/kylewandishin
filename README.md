## What I Do

I build systems that make work feel lighter.

Not giving you another dashboard.  

Not by adding new steps to your process.  

But, by removing the parts that shouldn’t exist in the first place.


## Right Now

I’m building Arterial — turning everyday driving into infrastructure intelligence.

- Roads are observed passively
- Hazards are detected automatically
- Depth is estimated without LiDAR
- Cities get prioritized, actionable work

No surveys. No manual audits. No wasted time and money.

## How I Think

Skilled people should work on skilled work. We should be creating systems to make this happen and push the world to a better place where the amazing skilled people we work with are not being held back by mundane tedious tasks.
